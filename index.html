TOKENOMICS

DISTRIBUTION DETAILS

Total supply : 10,000,000 $CAMP tokens
Mining (68%) : 6,800,000 tokens locked until mainnet.
Campspace (20%): 2,000,000 tokens
Private Sale/IDO (10%): 1,000,000 tokens
Team (2%): 200,000 tokens locked for 2 years

Mining

68% of the total supply is mineable and will be unlocked on mainnet launch. Hosting node holders will receive mined tokens for each block depending on their percentage of total capacity offered on campfire protocol and average uptime. This token pool is locked until mainnet launch.

CAMPSPACE

20 %of the total supply is used for building campfire camps-pace. Splitting this ecosystem pool, 5% will be used for private Sale/lDO campaign listing and liquidity pools funding; 15% will be used to incentivize strategic partnership, bounties for external developers participating in contributing in our opensource campspace and incentivize governance participation.

PRIVATE SALE/IDO

10% of the total supply is used for fundraising through a private Sale Or IDO (will be announced soon). Funds raised will be used to recruit software developers to strengthen campfire team and contribute to our marketing needs.

TEAM

2% of the total supply is used for fundraising through a private Sale Or IDO (will be announced soon). Funds raised will be used to recruit software developers to strengthen campfire team and contribute to our marketing needs.
